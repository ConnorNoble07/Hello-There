Connor's Top 5 Love Songs:

I know you might not like all of these, some country and some rock. But this is just a side page, even if you don't accept my proposition, I hope you enjoy some of the music.



5: November Rain By: Guns N' Roses

Link

A great song by one of my favorite bands about falling in love, and probably more I don't understand.



4: More Hearts Than Mine By: Ingrid Andress

Link

A really good song from a few years back, but I can never remember the singers name.



3: Forever and Ever Amen By: Randy Travis

Link

A song that I first heard because of my dad, despite this, it's still a top song in my book.



2: Can't Fight This Feeling By: REO Speedwagon

Link

A song about going from friends to more. Good song, worth a listen.



1: Your Song By: Elton John

Link

A pretty standard sounding 70's love song, but something about it speaks to me in a way most other love songs haven't.
